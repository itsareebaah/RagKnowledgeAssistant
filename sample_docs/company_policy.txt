ACME Corp — Employee Handbook (excerpt)

Vacation Policy
New full-time employees receive 15 paid vacation days per year, starting after a 90-day probation period.
After 5 years of service, vacation increases to 20 days per year.

Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
All remote workers must be available during core hours: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM local time.

Refund Policy (B2B customers)
Enterprise customers may request a full refund within 30 days of purchase if onboarding was not completed.
Refunds after 30 days require VP approval.

Support Hours
Technical support is available Monday–Friday, 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM EST.
Emergency support for Enterprise tier: 24/7 via the dedicated hotline.
